You can use this scatter plot to see the correlation between the markup of a listing and various attributes.

  • View individual listings or aggregate them into neighborhoods. When viewing neighborhoods, the size of the circle represents the number of listings in that neighborhood.
  • Use the dropdown menu along the horizontal axis to choose an attribute to plot against the markup.
  • Zoom in and out using scroll controls, and pan around using Alt + Drag.
  • You can Shift + Click to select multiple listings or neighborhoods. You may also drag over a region to select all listings or neighborhoods under it.

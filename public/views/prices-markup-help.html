The price and markup blocks show the distribution of daily rental price as well as markup for the current selection. The blocks are sized by the number of listings in that range.

  • You can click on a block to reveal the individual listings inside, as well as to highlight those listings in the scatter plot.
  • You can click on an individual listing within a selected block — for example, a price block — to find its corresponding value on the markup block.
  • You can Shift + Click to select multiple price blocks or markup blocks.

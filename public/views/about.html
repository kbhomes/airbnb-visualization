is an interactive visualization that strives to compare the prices of listings with estimates to see price markups in San Francisco.

is an online platform that enables people to rent houses or rooms, while is a real estate website that, among others, lists estimated rental prices for a given area.

With our visualization, a tourist can make a better-informed decision on where they choose to live and avoid being a victim of price gouging. Residents of San Francisco can understand the presence of units and their prices across different neighborhoods. An analyst, can study how different factors may affect the markup value of a listing. Finally, potential hosts can have a baseline comparison for pricing their rentals.
